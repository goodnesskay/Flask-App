Services

Here are the service Norman Platform offers you.

Appointments/Scheduling

This service allows patients to fix appointment at hospital.

Real-Time Messaging

This service provides a real time messaging interface between patient's social media platform and hospital.

Inventory

This services allows hospital keep track of patients registered under them.



Form Filling

With Norman bot, bye to boring forms as the bot provides you a more interactive and fun-filled platform of filling forms.

Registration

You can register under an hospital via Norman bot. All you need do is select your location and the hospital you want to register under.

Location

Norman helps track your location and also helps you in locating health centers close to you, the direction to your hospital and health centers in other cities.

Drug Detailing

Get to know about the drug you want to use before taking them through Norman bot.

Reminder

This service handles all reminder related events. It tries to unburden your patient from excessively remembering some things by providing the following features:

  • Drug use Reminder
  • Appointment Reminders
  • User Specific Reminders
